Want to be a part of the Veldivia team? View our current openings.

Our Team

Veldivia is a brand-new project that truly kicked off on the 7th, and we’re already working hard to make it something special. We love taking ideas and turning them into real experiences in Minecraft, and we’re looking for people who feel the same way. If you’re driven, excited to help build a server from its early days, and ready to put real effort into the community, there’s a place for you here. All roles are fully remote, and compensation varies depending on the position.

General Expectations

All applicants should meet the following base requirements:

  • Strong English language skills for effective written and spoken communication
  • Access to reliable tools and equipment for remote team collaboration
  • Ability to work across different time zones, especially US/EU business hours
  • Background in remote work environments or technology-focused companies
  • Authentic enthusiasm for digital innovation and crafting quality user experiences

Why Join Veldivia

Every role at Veldivia comes with these core benefits:

  • Remote work with flexible hours - Enjoy the freedom to work from anywhere with a schedule that adapts to your life
  • Interact with a large and growing community - Become part of an active network of players, moderators, creators, and developers
  • Opportunities to improve your skills and grow personally - Gain hands-on experience, learn new skills, and advance at your own pace
  • Work with a motivated and dedicated team - Collaborate with a passionate staff focused on creating a welcoming and well-managed environment
